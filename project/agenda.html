Agenda

  • February 5

    5:00 pm - 8:00 pm

    Expo floor will be open. Food and drinks will be provided

  • February 6

    8:00 am - 11:30 am

    Farmer panels that will be discussing how the previous year went for them. This will be the opportunity for people to ask questions and learn how other customers are using data in their operations. There will also be 3 breakout sessions that will give opportunities to ask more specific questions to each of the presenters

  • 12:00 pm - 1:00 pm

    Lunch will be provided.

  • 1:00 pm - 2:00 pm

    Dan Paschke is the Director of Marketing Solutions as a service at John Deere. Dan will be presenting on the latest technology from John Deere.

  • 2:00 pm - 5:00 pm

    The second set of farmer panels that will be discussing how the previous year went for them. This will be the opportunity for people to ask questions and learn how other customers are using data in their operations. There will also be 3 breakout sessions that will give opportunities to ask more specific questions to each of the presenters

  • 5:30 pm - 6:00 pm

    Bus transportation provided to Atomic Golf. First bus will leave the SouthPoint hotel at 5:30. The last bus will leave promptly at 6:00 pm. Be sure to get on any of the busses.

  • 6:00 pm - 9:00 pm

    Atomic Golf will be open for attendees to play golf and have dinner. Drinks and dinner will be provided.
